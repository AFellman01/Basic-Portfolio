It is a far, far better thing that I do, than I have ever done; it is a far, far better rest that I go to than I have ever known. Our doubts are traitors, and make us lose the good we might have won, by fearing to attempt. All we have to decide is what to do with the time that is given to us.
I have a lovely bunch of coconuts, there they are standing in a row. Big ones, small ones, some as big as your head. Give them a twist, a flick of the wrist, that's what the the showman said. Yes, we have no bananas, we have no bananas today. Grapes are eaten one by one. I hope I never smell the smell of apples again.
It is a truth universally acknowledged, that a single man in possession of a good fortune, must be in want of a wife. This is the saddest story that I have ever heard. Happy families are all alike; every unhappy family is unhappy in its own way. The sun shone, having no alternative.
